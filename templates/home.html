the challenge

Interview anxiety? Feeling unprepared? Many struggle with confidence and practice, especially in intimidating mock interviews. Effective preparation is often hard to come by.

our AI solution

Interview Ready provides a non-judgmental, AI-powered space to practice. Get tailored questions, instant feedback, and intelligent scoring to build skills and confidence comfortably.

How Interview Ready Works

1. select domain

Choose your job field (IT, Management, Civil Services, etc.) to tailor the practice session.

2. add resume (optional)

Upload your CV for highly personalized questions based on your experience (or skip this step).

3. practice & respond

Engage in a mock interview with our AI, answering domain-specific questions.

4. get feedback & score

Receive instant, constructive feedback and an intelligent score (out of 10) for each answer.
