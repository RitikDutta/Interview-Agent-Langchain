Context

The problem: interviews are scary, practice shouldn’t be.

Traditional mock interviews with real people can feel intimidating — especially for shy, first-time, or under-confident candidates. Many never get enough realistic practice before the real exam day.

The Problem

Many individuals struggle with interview preparation. Whether due to shyness, inexperience, or lack of confidence, traditional mock interviews with real people can feel intimidating and high-pressure.

• Fear of being judged during practice interviews.
• No safe space to make mistakes and learn.
• Busy mentors can’t run repeated mock sessions.
• Feedback is often vague or delayed.

The Solution

Interview Mentor is a revolutionary AI-powered application that uses generative AI to create a secure, private space for mock interviews. It simulates realistic conversations, provides end-to-end feedback, and uses an intelligent scoring system to evaluate performance while saving progress for personalized practice.

Flow

How Interview Ready works — from hello to hired.

The experience is designed to feel like a real interview, while the AI quietly handles session management, scoring, and personalization behind the scenes.

Step-by-step experience

  1. Pick your domain & preferences. Choose IT, business management, psychology, civil services and more. Select interviewer gender, language (English / Hindi) and difficulty level.
  2. Upload your resume (optional). The system reads your CV to tailor questions to your background, projects, and skills. Users without a CV can still proceed.
  3. Talk to your AI interviewer. The AI asks domain-specific questions. You respond just like in a real conversation via text or voice.
  4. Get instant scores & coaching. Every response is scored out of 10 with clear feedback on clarity, relevance, and technical accuracy, plus tips for improvement.

Non-judgmental practice

Practice as many times as you like. Restart questions, explore follow-ups, and experiment with answers without worrying what the interviewer thinks.

Intelligent scoring system

Answers are scored on clarity, depth, structure, and relevance. Non-applicable cases are gracefully handled so the JSON responses stay consistent and reliable.

Progress tracking

Every session is saved to your profile in Firebase Firestore. You can revisit past interviews, compare scores, and visually see how your confidence grows over time.

Features & stack

What makes Interview Ready different.

AI-powered mock interviews

Tailored questions and feedback based on the user’s domain and background, powered by OpenAI models and custom prompts.

Personalized experience

Users can create profiles with preferences for interviewer gender, language (English / Hindi), and desired career domain, making each session feel uniquely theirs.

High-level security

Google authentication, secure session management, and Firestore rules ensure user data and interview history stay protected.

Real interview simulation

From ice-breakers to deep technical follow-ups, the flow mirrors actual interviews across domains such as IT, business management, psychology, and civil services.

Smart scoring

Responses are scored out of 10 with intelligent handling of non-applicable scenarios, keeping the evaluation fair and the JSON output clean.

Cloud-native deployment

Flask-based backend, Firebase Cloud Firestore, and Google Cloud hosting make the system reliable, scalable, and easy to extend.
